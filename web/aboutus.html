About Us!

CakeShop.com is a cake manufacturing company featuring premium quality, frozen, fully finished packaged cake products for the Food Service, Catering, C-store and In-Store Bakery.We specialize in creating unique, and custom, high-quality, finished cakes for a variety of operators that are not generally found in ready-to-sell formats.

Creative Occasions cakes are produced by sourcing the finest quality of ingredients in every aspect of our operation while carefully balancing the price - value relationship for the in-store bakery cake category and consumer.

What makes our cakes taste so good? CakeShop.com has been helping people express themselves through Birthday Cakes.Our experience is unmatched and our reputation firmly established. We have a wide and established dealer and supplier network that allows us to supply the best quality of Cakes for you. The Internet had given us the opportunity to serve a wider clientele more efficiently so we embarked on this medium to provide our benchmark quality and service to the global flower lovers.
